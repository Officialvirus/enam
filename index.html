About e-NAM

The National Agriculture Market (e-NAM) is an online trading platform for agricultural commodities in India. It integrates APMCs and enables farmers to sell their produce transparently.

Learn More

Stakeholders

Farmers

Farmers can sell their produce online to get better market rates.

Traders

Traders can buy quality produce at competitive prices.

APMCs

APMCs help regulate and facilitate agricultural trade.

e-NAM Mandis

Currently, over 1,000 mandis across India are integrated with e-NAM.

  • Uttar Pradesh - 150 Mandis
  • Maharashtra - 70 Mandis
  • Madhya Pradesh - 58 Mandis
  • ... and many more
